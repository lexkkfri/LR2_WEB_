About Ordo

Ordo is an online service for tracking daily progress in work and study, helping you organize workflows, control deadlines, and maintain personal productivity.

With Ordo, you can keep a daily journal, create and track to-do lists, use focus timers, and monitor your work regularity through daily check-ins.

Features

To-Do Lists & Deadlines

Create and track tasks with deadlines to stay organized and never miss important dates.

Daily Journal

Keep notes and track your daily progress to build productive habits.

Focus Timer

Use the Pomodoro timer to maintain concentration during work sessions.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your work regularity with daily check-ins and streak counters.

Advantages

All-in-One Solution

Combine tasks, journaling, and focus timer in one product without needing separate apps.

Simple & Intuitive

Focus on Productivity

Tools specifically designed to help you build better work habits and stay focused.
